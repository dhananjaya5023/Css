Cubbon Park

In the urban jungle of Bangalore, is a green and pleasant refuge lined with thousands of trees and fragrant flowers. Known as Cubbon Park, this lush green area is rightly called the ‘Lungs of Bangalore’ and is home to hundreds of birds as well.

From morning joggers to evening runners and from weekend family outings to relaxing walks on weekdays, Cubbon Park is one little world amidst the urban chaos of the metro city that provides something to everyone. The park was established in 1879 and now imbibes 300 acres of refreshing and rejuvenating environment. However, there is much more to it when it comes to things to do in Cubbon Park. It is a perfect weekend destination for an outing with friends, family and even solitary soul-searching and relaxation
